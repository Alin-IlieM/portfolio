About Me

Hello, my name is Alin, a programming enthusiast from Cluj-Napoca.

My journey started when I enrolled in a Java course at FastTrackIt here in Cluj-Napoca, I finished the course, got my Analyst Programmer diploma and soon started working as a Web Scraper

Time passed and the 2020 pandemic hit, the company I was working for figured out that they don't need web scrapers anymore and our department got dissolved

Due to the fact that I had to extract information from websites most of the time, I got accustomed to how they work on a basic level and figured out that it would be quite easy and enjoyable for me to work as a Front-End Developer, so during the pandemic, up until now, I used the time to study HTML, CSS, Javascript and React
